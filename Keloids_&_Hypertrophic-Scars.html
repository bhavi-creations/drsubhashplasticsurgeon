Keloids & Hypertrophic Scars

Dr. Subhash Center for Plastic Surgery in Kakinada offers a comprehensive range of services to help individuals achieve their desired appearance. One of the services offered by the center is treatment for keloids and hypertrophic scars.

Keloids and hypertrophic scars are thick, raised scars that can be caused by an injury, surgery, or skin condition. They can be unsightly and may cause discomfort, itching, and even pain. At Dr. Subhash Center for Plastic Surgery, we offer a variety of treatments for keloids and hypertrophic scars to help improve their appearance and reduce symptoms.

Our treatments include:

  • Steroid Injections: Steroid injections are a common treatment for keloids and hypertrophic scars. The injections help to reduce inflammation and flatten the scar.

  • Laser Therapy: Laser therapy can be used to treat keloids and hypertrophic scars. The laser helps to break down the scar tissue and stimulate the growth of new, healthy tissue.

  • Cryotherapy: Cryotherapy involves freezing the scar tissue to help reduce its size and thickness.

  • Surgical Excision: Surgical excision is a procedure where the keloid or hypertrophic scar is surgically removed. This is typically done for larger scars or scars that are causing significant discomfort.
