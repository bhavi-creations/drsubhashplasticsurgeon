Tattoo Removal

Dr. Subhash Center For Plastic Surgery in Kakinada offers state-of-the-art tattoo removal services for patients looking to remove unwanted tattoos. Using advanced technology, our experienced team can help you achieve a clean, clear skin with minimal scarring.

Here are some of the tattoo removal services offered by Dr. Subhash Center For Plastic Surgery:

  • Laser Tattoo Removal: This is the most common method used to remove tattoos. It involves using a laser to break down the ink particles in the tattoo, which are then absorbed by the body and naturally eliminated. The number of sessions required depends on the size, color, and depth of the tattoo.
  • Surgical Excision: This method involves surgically removing the tattooed skin and suturing the surrounding skin together. This technique is best suited for smaller tattoos.
  • Dermabrasion: This method involves using a high-speed rotary device to remove the top layers of skin, along with the tattoo. This method is best suited for smaller tattoos and may require multiple sessions.

At Dr. Subhash Center For Plastic Surgery, our team of experts will work with you to determine the best tattoo removal method for your individual needs. We understand that tattoos can be a source of regret and emotional distress for many patients, and we are committed to helping you achieve the best possible results. We use the latest techniques and technology to ensure that your tattoo removal is safe, effective, and minimally invasive.

If you are interested in removing a tattoo, contact us today to schedule a consultation with one of our experts. We will discuss your options and help you achieve the clear, smooth skin you desire.
