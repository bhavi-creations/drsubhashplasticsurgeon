What is VERVE HIPRO-V LIFTING ?

VERVE Hipro-V uses precise medical ultrasound to tighten and lift skin non-surgically. It stimulates collagen production by delivering focused ultrasound energy to the SMAS layer and deep dermis area, without causing any damage to surrounding skin.

The contracted SMAS layer allows patients to experience some level of tightening and lifting immediately after the treatment. However, the majority of result starts appearing gradually over the next 2-3 months as part of the body's natural healing process that leads to formation of new collagen.
