Hand and Foot Deformities

Hand and foot deformities can occur as a result of injury, birth defects, or degenerative conditions. At Dr. Subhash Center for Plastic Surgery in Kakinada, we specialize in the treatment of hand and foot deformities and offer comprehensive care to help patients improve their function and quality of life.

Our team of skilled plastic surgeons and medical professionals uses the latest technology and techniques to provide effective treatment for hand and foot deformities. We understand the unique challenges that these conditions present and work closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that address their specific needs.

Our hand and foot deformities treatment services include:

  • Hand Reconstruction Surgery: Our plastic surgeons specialize in hand reconstruction surgery, which involves repairing and reconstructing the bones, tendons, and soft tissues of the hand to restore function and appearance.

  • Foot Reconstruction Surgery: We offer foot reconstruction surgery, which involves treating injuries or deformities of the foot, including fractures, dislocations, and bone or soft tissue loss.

  • Hand Therapy: Our team of occupational therapists provides hand therapy, including exercises and splinting, to improve hand function and dexterity.

  • Foot Therapy: We offer foot therapy, including exercises and custom orthotics, to improve foot function and reduce pain.

At Dr. Subhash Center for Plastic Surgery, we are committed to providing the highest quality of care to patients with hand and foot deformities. We offer ongoing support and care throughout the treatment process, including follow-up visits, counseling services, and support groups.

If you or a loved one has a hand or foot deformity, contact Dr. Subhash Center for Plastic Surgery in Kakinada to schedule a consultation with our experienced plastic surgeons and medical professionals. We are here to help you improve your function and quality of life.
